What makes someone influential or inspirational in your life?

There are many different ways a person could be an inspiration in your life. Here are some quotes from some influential people: 

“If something is important enough, even if the odds are stacked against you, you should still do it.” – Elon Musk

“If you’re offered a seat on a rocket ship, don’t ask what seat! Just get on.” – Sheryl Sandberg

“I always did something I was a little not ready to do. I think that’s how you grow. When there’s that moment of ‘Wow, I’m not really sure I can do this,’ and you push through those moments, that’s when you have a breakthrough.” – Marissa Mayer

“You can waste your lives drawing lines. Or you can live your life crossing them.” – Shonda Rhimes

“Success is liking yourself, liking what you do, and liking how you do it.” – Maya Angelou

 

I find that the most influential people in my life, are my family, especially my 6 year baby girl.  The reason my family members are the most influential people in my life is because they display true love and kindess towards me. Even when we have hard times, we are there for one another.  The good times and the bad times!

I also find inspiration in "famous" people.  I provided links (on left hand side) for you to read more about these influential people. We all have different reasons for finding someone influential. Who was the last person who has influenced you? Why?
